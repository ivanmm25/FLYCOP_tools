FLYCOP PIPELINE FOR EVALUATION AND SELECTION OF CONSORTIUM ARCHITECTURE
***NARINGENIN DECORATION WITH GERANYL***
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Consortium: E. coli iEC1364W, P.putida iJN1463
----------

Metabolic roles in a 2-model consortium.

	* Fructose and p-coumarate production, naringenin decoration with geranyl: E.coli iEC1364W.
	* Malon and naringenin production, cobalamin production: P.putida iJN1463.
	
Metabolic roles in a 3-model consortium.

	* Fructose and p-coumarate production: E.coli iEC1364W.
	* Naringenin decoration with geranyl: E.coli iEC1364W (decorator strain, non-fructose producer).
	* Malon and naringenin production, cobalamin production: P.putida iJN1463.
